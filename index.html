Profile

I try to find creativity in everything I do. When I can't find it in what I'm doing, I do it on the side. My passion projects started as ideas and have developed into the pieces I am most proud of.

It started with GLOW. For my Masters thesis I explored behvaiour changing technologies and positivity online. GLOW was a digital social cause bracelet, a wearable that fought cyberbullying amongst young girls. It was optioned by a production company and is currently in pitching stages to continue its development.

In 2015 I co-founded a creative initiative that runs design-sprint workshops with high school students (Gen Z). Studio Bud was inspired by the creative nature of play, we wanted to arm students with the creative confidence to come up with ideas and share them! We’ve developed a unique process that starts with playful ideation and ends in refining and focusing of ideas. We encourage fearlessness in idea development, something I wish I had at 16 years old. Over the past 3 years we have attracted companies like Canon Canada and Cineplex in harnessing this creativity in youth to help them solve problems and come up with ideas.

I currently work as the Digital Branding & Content Manager at California Innovations. I handle their digital ads, digital content development and optimization, the company’s social media presence, digital strategy and help run arcticzone.com.
